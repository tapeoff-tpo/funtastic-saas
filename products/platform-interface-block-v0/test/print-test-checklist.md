## Use Feel

Record 1-5 only after direct comparison: assembly fun, engagement satisfaction,
required force, force consistency, rattle, play, scratching, friction noise,
Base movement and fear of breakage.

## Cycle Checkpoints

Complete at 0, 10, 25, 50 and 100 attach/remove cycles for every interface.
Record attach force, removal force, play, whitening, cracks, debris, engagement
failure and accidental release. Descriptive force language is acceptable until
an instrument and method are fixed.

## Low-Load Check

Use the same low load, position, duration and sequence. Record vertical load,
horizontal pull, twist, and load retention after 100 cycles. This is not a
safety or high-load qualification.
