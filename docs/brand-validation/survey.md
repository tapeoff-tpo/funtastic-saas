# FUN-TASTIC Brand Validation Survey

Status: draft instrument; no responses collected

## Administration Rules

- Randomly assign one message condition and one offer condition.
- Do not show the FUN-TASTIC brand name, preferred internal concept, or all alternatives before primary measures.
- Require an unaided text response immediately after exposure.
- Keep images, timing, item count, and information density controlled as specified in the test documents.
- Record device type, language, completion time, and assigned cell.
- Pilot before fixing decision sample size.

## Section A: Eligibility and Behavior

### Q1. Recent organizing action

지난 12개월 동안 작은 생활용품을 정리하거나 보관하기 위해 직접 구매하거나 설치한 제품이 있나요?

- Yes, purchased
- Yes, installed or improvised without purchasing
- Considered but did not purchase
- No

Primary evidence requires one of the first three responses. Record the difference.

## Data Quality Flags

Flag rather than silently delete:

- Completion time below the pilot-defined minimum
- Failed attention or comprehension check
- Contradictory responses
- Duplicate participant or device
- Employee or project collaborator
- Missing required free-text response
- Copy-pasted or nonsensical text

Exclusion rules must be approved before the decision test.
