# Fit Coupon Test Checklist

Results must remain blank until the physical print is inspected.

## Print inspection

- Print date:
- Printer:
- PLA brand / color / lot:
- Filament condition:
- Actual time / weight:
- Support used: No
- Brim used:
- Socket floor quality:
- Retention bead completeness:
- Plug guide and pocket completeness:
- Warping or lifted corners:

## Forced test order

1. Test Light first.
2. Record every Light result.
3. Test Standard.
4. Record every Standard result.
5. Test Firm only if Light and Standard are too loose or if safe removal remains credible.
6. Stop Firm immediately for excessive force, whitening, squeaking, cracking, or severe Socket flex.

## Per-fit record

- First insertion possible:
- Fully seated using fingers only:
- Straight Push-In maintained:
- Tactile completion:
- Click sound:
- Partial seating:
- Play:
- Wobble:
- Pull-Out possible:
- Coupon Base lifts:
- Scratching:
- Whitening:
- Wear dust:
- Retention bead damage:
- Socket-wall damage:

## Cycle checkpoints

Record at 0, 10, 25, 50, and 100 cycles:

- Insertion and removal feel
- Play and wobble change
- Whitening or cracks
- Wear dust
- Bead, pocket, and Socket damage
- Accidental release

## Reject

- Cannot seat by hand or requires a tool
- Cannot Pull-Out
- Crack or severe whitening on first engagement
- Partial seating is mistaken for completion
- Fit changes sharply before 25 cycles
- Accidental release before 100 cycles
- Structural damage to Socket or Plug

## Candidate retained

- Natural one-hand or two-hand insertion and removal
- Completion distinguishable by touch or sound
- Acceptable play
- Function retained after 100 cycles
- Only minor PLA damage
- Suitable for another color and filament-lot verification

Do not select Light, Standard, or Firm until this evidence is recorded.
