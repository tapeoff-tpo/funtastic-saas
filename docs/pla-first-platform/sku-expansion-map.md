# PLA First Platform - SKU Expansion Map

Checked: 2026-07-18

## Three-year 100-SKU Capacity

One hundred catalog entries are technically possible, but 100 profitable SKUs are not yet proven. The target below is a capacity map, not a release commitment.

| Portfolio branch | Three-year capacity | Evidence basis | PLA boundary |
|---|---:|---|---|
| Core base, expansion and starter sets | 10 | Every platform requires acquisition, expansion and replacement paths | Indoor, low-load only |
| General desk organization | 15 | Broad marketplace demand for trays, cups, stands and small bins | No monitor/laptop structural supports |
| Stationery and craft | 15 | Object-specific tools and consumables create repeat module demand | Avoid sharp-tool safety claims |
| Gaming setup | 15 | Controller/headphone stands and personalization have paid demand | No electronics, heat or licensed character dependency |
| Collectible and hobby display | 15 | Collections grow over time and need repeat risers/stands | Low-height/lightweight display only |
| Camera and creator desk | 10 | Battery/card/accessory holders form a model-specific long tail | Desktop storage, not field protection |
| 3D-printer maintenance desk | 10 | Strong maker demand for tools, clips and consumable organization | Keep away from hot-end/chamber heat zones |
| Dry vanity organization | 5 | Color-led modular sets and repeat cosmetics organization | Dry countertop only; no shower zone |
| Dry coffee organization | 5 | Strong accessory demand but material boundary is narrow | No hot, wet or food-contact use |
| **Total capacity** | **100** |  |  |

## Expansion Gates

Each branch must pass all of these gates before more SKUs are added:

1. The module solves a use case not already covered by another SKU.
2. At least one prior module in the branch has repeat-order or bundle-attach evidence.
3. Return and fit complaints are within the target set by the business before launch.
4. The module remains inside PLA temperature, load and environment boundaries.
5. Contribution margin remains positive after printing, inspection, personalization, packing and shipping.

## PETG Expansion After Validation

PETG is not required for the first platform. If introduced later, it expands the addressable boundary rather than replacing the PLA core:

- Humid bathroom modules outside direct continuous water exposure
- More flexible clips and snap-intensive accessories
- Higher-impact cable and under-desk modules
- Camera field cases and workshop accessories with moderate impact
- Coffee-zone parts closer to moisture, while still avoiding hot surfaces and food-contact claims without testing
- Light garage organization where ambient heat and impact exceed the PLA boundary

Automotive interiors, outdoor exposure, high loads, and safety-critical products remain outside the automatic PETG expansion assumption.
