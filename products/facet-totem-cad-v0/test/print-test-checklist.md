## Repeated engagement

Record at 0, 10, 25, 50, and 100 cycles:

- Change in seating feel
- Change in play or wobble
- Whitening
- Cracks
- Wear dust
- Retention-pocket deformation
- Base-bead or socket deformation
- Accidental release

## Stop conditions

- Stop Firm testing if insertion requires tools, extreme force, visible cracking, or lifts the Base uncontrollably.
- Stop cycle testing if cracks propagate or fragments detach.
- Do not treat the prototype as a safety-critical or high-load product.
